About Us

Lady bug is a non-commercial software product that can be licensed for running on-premises or available as a hosted application.

Our team provides Lady bug for free to open source projects meeting certain criteria, and to organizations that are non-academic, non-commercial, non-governmental, non-political, non-profit, and secular. For academic and commercial customers,the full source code is available under a developer source license.
